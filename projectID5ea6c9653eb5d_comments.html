A history of the Mongols, who in medieval times conquered most of Asia, by the well-known American ethnographer, folklorist, and translator, Jeremiah Curtin, who traveled to Russia and the Far East to collect material for this work. A companion work, The Mongols in Russia is also in preparation. The works where published after his death, and appear to have been significantly edited by his wife, Alma Cardell Curtin.

No particular difficulties in this text, so have fun!
